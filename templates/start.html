Welcome to the Crop Planter Guide by Harvest Date

This tool helps you decide what to plant today based on desired harvest dates

Instructions: Enter two dates below between which you want to be able to obtain your First harvest. Naturally more days between your dates/in your range result in more crops.

After submitting (response times are typically 20 seconds, but can be up to a minute) you will see a table including:
  • Crop Name
  • Wikipedia Link
  • Date you should first expect to harvest
  • Date you should expect last harvest
  • Perinnial or not (Annual)
