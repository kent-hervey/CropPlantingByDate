Welcome to the Crop Planter Guide by Harvest Date

This tool helps you decide what to plant today based on desired harvest dates

After submitting (response times are typically less than 20 seconds, but can be up to a minute) you will see a table including (assuming planting date of today):
  • Crop Name
  • Wikipedia Link
  • Date you should first expect to harvest
  • Date you should expect last harvest
  • Perinnial or not (Annual)

This tool uses source data from http://growstuff.org/
Results are based on expected time to harvest in days.
Weather, sunlight for your location/time of year, soil/air temperatures are not factored.
